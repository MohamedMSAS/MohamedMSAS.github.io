Budgie's own little world

Budgies are little birds that are commonly kept as pets inside of many households, although some are roaming around in australia, they are mostly pets.

More About Budgies

Budgie Colors

Budgies come in a small variety of colors, being only yellow/green, blue and white, although yellow/green is the only natural budgie color, other colors are for mutations for pet shops.

Types of Budgies
